## Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

### Types of Data Collected

#### Personal Data

While using Our Service, We may ask You to provide Us with certain personally identifiable information. This may include, but is not limited to:

- Email address
- First name and last name
- Profile photo (optional)

#### Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service. This may include information such as Your device type, iOS version, unique device identifiers, IP address and other diagnostic data.

#### Location Data

While using Our Application, and with Your prior permission, We collect your location in order to:

- Show nearby karting venues on the map
- Verify that You are physically present at a venue when checking in

Location data is only used while the app is in use. It is never sold, shared with third parties for advertising purposes, or stored beyond what is necessary to provide the above features. You can enable or disable access to location at any time through Your device Settings.

## Children's Privacy

GoKarting is designed to be used by karters of all ages, including those under 13, as karting is a family and junior sport. The app does not include any messaging, social networking, or user-to-user communication features.

We do not knowingly collect more Personal Data from minors than is necessary to provide the Service (account registration and check-in functionality). We do not sell, share, or use data collected from users under 13 for advertising or any purpose beyond providing the core features of the app.

If You are a parent or guardian and You are aware that Your child has provided Us with Personal Data beyond what is described above, please contact Us and We will take steps to remove that information from Our servers.
