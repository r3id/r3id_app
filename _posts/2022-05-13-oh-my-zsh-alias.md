So on a recent call at work i discovered somehting really cool about [Oh My ZSH](https://ohmyz.sh). 

_"What is this cool thing?"_ I hear you ask.

Well that would be the `alias` command. 

The `alias` commands provide you with handy shortcuts to certain actions such as...

```
ga='git add'
gp='git push'
gco='git checkout'
gcmsg='git commit -m'
glog='git log --oneline --decorate --graph'
```

It well worth exploring this great litle command.
