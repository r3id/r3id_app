**ibrandIQ as Tecnical Director** _July 2018 ~ July 2019_

Working at ibrandIQ, I was brought in to maintain their existing where-to-buy platform. This quickly moved to into developing the next version of the platform.

The new platform organised products from a global catalogue of retailers, in multiple languages. It also took a different approach to the where-to-buy solution which made it far more flexible than the previous system. The outcome meant there were less daily admin tasks, which allowed the team to work on other more critical projects.

**Bud. as UI Developer** _July 2017 ~ Feb 2018_

I joined Bud to head up their front-end team, and move the web app forward. I worked closely with the in-house design team; the goal was to design and develop a functional and user-friendly platform which would enable users to aggregate all their financial data.

**Capital on Tap as Senior UI Developer** _February 2017 ~ July 2017_

I was asked to return to Capital on Tap as they needed someone to create a living style guide and develop guidelines for their front end projects. While there I worked on some public facing pages to help promote the company and build there online presence.

**Capital on Tap as Developer** _April 2015 ~ April 2016_

I primarily worked on front-end projects optimising, reworking and improving load times to the public facing sites. I also led the creation and implementation of a custom SASS library into existing web solutions projects and training members of the team on how to use the library.

During my time at Capital on Tap, I also had exposure to some of the .NET back end. Including working on unit tests, developing the customer portals so users could manage their accounts online.

**Balmoral Financial as Senior Developer** _November 2014 ~ March 2015_

I refactored the company site by rewriting most of the code on the front end. These changes led to improvements in the UI, and performance of the site overall.

**Fasttrack Company as Developer** _April 2014 ~ October 2014_

I designed and developed the UI concept for the user and admin areas which were built using .NET MVC, and WebAPI. Along with the admin areas, I also created a conceptual native Android application. This app used Bluetooth LE to communicate with prototype devices and transferred data to the prototype.

**NASDAQ as Senior Designer/Developer** _October 2012 ~ March 2014_

During my time at NASDAQ, I was the only designer/developer on the team. Utilising the in-house framework, I developed newsroom sites for various big brand clients such as Facebook, Sony, Gatwick, Mattel and Centre Parcs.

I also designed responsive, eye-catching designs for clients including Sony, and Gatwick. Two of which were put forward for 'design impact' awards.

Also, while working at NASDAQ, I became a Microsoft Certified Professional.

**The Whisky Exchange as Front-end developer** _May 2012 ~ October 2012_

I designed and developed an internal system which allowed the sales team to manage products and orders on Amazon using the MWS API. It was created using .NET 4.0 which ran LINQ queries and web services to process data, and communicate with the Amazon API. I also worked on many minor front-end projects, micro-sites, newsletters, cross browser compatibility.
