After enjoying an evening out with friends, don't let the tedious task of splitting the cost of the bill ruin the evening. Bill Split! is an easy to use calculator which gets the job done for you in a few seconds.

Simply enter the amount of the bill, the number of people, and a tip percentage and the app will calculate the amount that each individual would need to pay. It really is that simple!
