Back in 2013 when I was working at NASDAQ OMX, I had the privilege of developing and doing a little bit of design work on the Facebook Newsroom.

Using NASDAQ's newsroom platform, I helped to develop new sections of the newsroom site, which involved building up the styling to match the Facebook branding. It was a unique opportunity which I was pleased to be part of.
